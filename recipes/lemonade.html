Lemonade

Description

Who doesn't love a cool glass of lemonade in the summer season of India. In these exhausting periods of our summer the only thing that helps us relax after a long play outside is a sweet and cold glass of fresh lemons ready for you in your house.

Lemonade is a very popular drink in hot places specially countries located near the equator. Other than its refreshing ability this drink has many added benefits because of the presence of lemon to it.

Ingredients

  • Lemons
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Fill three-fourth of a glass with water.
  2. Squeeze the juices from a lemon into the glass.
  3. Add half a teaspoon of sugar to it.
  4. Add a pinch of salt to it.
