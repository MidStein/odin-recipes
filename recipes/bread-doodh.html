Bread Doodh

Description

This is one of my favorite dishes of all time even though it does not have much to it. I still get to eat it whenever I visit my parent's home and the habit of rolling the bread and stirring it in the milk and thus causing a mess has still not left me as an adult. This is highly recommended by me if you are hungry and do not want to go through the effort of making a recipe which takes more effort.

This dish combines the balanced nutrition of milk and the crunchy and solid quality of bread slices which makes it an enjoyable and at the same time nutritional substite for a children's meal occasionaly or in case the time is short. Since there are a very steps required to prepare this food, it can also be taught to children which parents will find very rewarding for both them and their children.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Melted Butter or Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a big bowl of milk until it starts bubbling.
  2. Let the milk cool
  3. Filter the cream out of the milk
  4. Take suitable number of bread slices
  5. Take a tablespoon of melted butter or oil and spread on a pan
  6. Put one slice of bread on the pan and move it on the surface till it gets sufficiently hot and then remove it
  7. Repeat with each slice of bread
  8. Add two tablespoons of sugar to the milk
