Cake

Description

Even a description would be unnecessary for such a world popular food item but still here we are. This chocoalate buttercream cake is going to make you expel so much saliva that we may end up adding a drink to our recipe list so that you don't unconsious out of thirst before you finish making this cake.

The cake has a very important part in our culture and society specially in the west. We even celebrate our aging process and our major festivals with large plates of cake. The cake has given birth to many more recipes because of its immense popularity. Some of these include pastries, cream cookies and ice creams.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 2 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 tablespoons milk

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients
  2. Add milk until frosting reaches spreadable consistency
